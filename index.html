Manic Miners: A LEGO Rock Raiders remake

DISCLAIMER: This project and site are in no way officially supported, endorsed, or recognized by The LEGO Group.

Manic Miners is a free remake of LEGO Rock Raiders being solo-developed by Baraklava. Development began in May 2019 and has continued since then! It features new content, countless improvements, and lots of surprises! Download the game down below for free or join the Discord to chat with other LEGO Rock Raiders and LEGO Media Games fans. If you have any questions about the game, check the FAQ as your question will mostly likely be answered!

This fresh Rock Raiders experience brings customizable hotkeys, extensive Rock Raider customization, Buildings, Vehicles, terrifying Creatures, a Level Editor, an extensive scripting system, lava erosion, landslides, spiders, extensive Eye/Shoulder view, and an abundance of new details, animations and secrets not in the original game!

Manic Miners aims to be what could’ve been if the franchise continued and subsequently released a “Rock Raiders 2” or “Rock Raiders remastered”, but 20 years later. All in the spirit of 90’s Lego themes and games. While performance and fun gameplay is the most important, all assets in the game are also supposed to be of highest quality: The models, the textures, the music, the animations… you name it, it is on the list. This is of course limited by my own ability as this is my first game. If you want to contribute in any way, my DMs and email are always open for suggestions.

Among other things, one big advantage of the game is a native Level Editor where you can realize the craziest dream levels you had when you played the original game! Dig in and see what you can create.

Alright, now I won’t hold you here for any longer! If you want to know more about the project in detail, head over to the FAQ!
